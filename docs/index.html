Hardware

The Playbook

Certified engineering decisions. Peer-reviewed by us, approved by no one.

Optical Shrouds

We slid heat-shrink tubing and cut straws over the photoresistors to block ambient light and sharpen line detection. In our heads, this is the same as designing a satellite sensor array.

High-Contrast Underglow

Ultra-bright white LEDs mounted under the chassis to overpower unpredictable room shadows. Technically robotics. Aesthetically Fast & Furious.

Traction Engineering

Wheels cleaned with rubbing alcohol. Coins placed directly over the drive axles for grip and weight distribution. The solution was coins. Just coins. It worked immediately.

Motor Noise Suppression

0.1µF ceramic capacitors soldered across motor terminals to filter electrical noise and stabilize analog sensor readings. We told the motors to stop complaining. They listened.

Exponential Outriders Protocol

When the outermost sensors of the V-shaped array detect the line, the error value is multiplied exponentially — triggering an aggressive emergency pivot. We call this "panic mode, but make it an algorithm." This one is actually in the code.
